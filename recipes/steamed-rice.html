Steamed Rice

Description

Steamed rice is the perfect, easy side-dish for any meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups White Rice
  • 4 cups Water

Steps

  1. Wash your white rice in the sink then drain.
  2. In a pot, add your rice and water.
  3. Cover and boil the rice at low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Let the rice rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Done!
