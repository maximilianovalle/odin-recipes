Steamed Rice

Description

Steamed rice is the perfect, easy side-dish for any meal!

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash 2 cups of white rice with water then drain.
  2. In a pot, add your rice and 4 cups of water.
  3. Cover and boil the rice at low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Let the rice rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Done!
