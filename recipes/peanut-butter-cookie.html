1-Minute Peanut Butter Cookie

Description

This is a delicious, quick, and easy recipe for satisfying your sweet tooth.

All you need are four simple ingredients and a mug!

Ingredients

  • 32 grams Peanut Butter
  • 1 (1/2) tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 2 tbsp Milk

Steps

  1. In one mug, add your peanut butter, brown sugar, flour, and milk.
  2. Stir the ingredients with a spoon.
  3. Microwave for 30-45 seconds depending on how doughy you'd like your cookie.
  4. Let cool and enjoy!
