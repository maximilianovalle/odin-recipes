1-Minute Peanut Butter Cookie

Description

This is a delicious, quick, and easy recipe for satisfying your sweet tooth.

All you need are four simple ingredients and a mug!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Milk

Steps

  1. In one mug, add 32 grams of peanut butter, 1 (1/2) tbsp of brown sugar, 2 tbsp of flour, and 2 tbsp of milk.
  2. Stir the ingredients with a spoon.
  3. Microwave for 30-45s depending on how doughy you'd like your cookie.
  4. Let cool and enjoy!
